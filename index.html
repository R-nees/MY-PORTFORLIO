About Me

I'm an aspiring web developer learning HTML, CSS, to create beautiful designs.

My journey into coding started with curiosity. As an architecture student, I always had an interest in design and structure, and I soon realized that web development offered a similar creative challenge. What excites me the most is the ability to bring ideas to life on a screen, crafting visually appealing and functional websites.

My motivation comes from seeing progress, whether it's completing a project, learning a new CSS trick, or making a design fully responsive. The process of improving and building something from scratch keeps me going. My goal is to continue learning, refining my skills, and eventually using web development to create impactful digital experiences.
